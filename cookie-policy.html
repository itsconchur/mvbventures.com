Cookie Policy

Last updated: 13/12/2025

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device to improve your browsing experience and help us understand how you use our website.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

a. Strictly Necessary Cookies

Required for the website to function (e.g., security, page navigation).
These cannot be disabled.

b. Performance & Analytics Cookies

Used to understand how visitors use the site (e.g., Google Analytics).
We only place these with your consent.

c. Functional Cookies

Enable enhanced features such as form preferences or saved settings.

d. Advertising & Targeting Cookies

Used to deliver personalised ads or measure advertising performance.
You must explicitly opt-in for these.

3. Why We Use Cookies

  • Improve website functionality
  • Analyse traffic and user behaviour
  • Personalise content
  • Deliver relevant advertising (if enabled)

4. Managing Cookies

You can:

  • Accept or reject non-essential cookies through our cookie banner
  • Disable cookies via your browser settings
  • Withdraw consent at any time

Blocking some cookies may affect site performance.

5. Third-Party Cookies

Our site may use third-party services such as:

  • Google Analytics
  • Meta Pixel
  • YouTube / Vimeo embeds
  • Advertising networks

These parties may set their own cookies in accordance with their privacy policies.

For any questions about cookies, contact: info@mvbventures.com
