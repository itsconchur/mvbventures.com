For founders & LPs

Why partner with us

MVB, not just MVP

We help teams move from idea or MVP to a Minimal Viable Business—repeatable GTM, disciplined unit economics, and reporting—so capital lands after a value-creation step we help shape. Founders gain CEO-level readiness; LPs get underwriting anchored in fundamentals, not hype.

Diligence others can trust

DNA-level diligence at operator depth means fewer surprises after we invest and a repeatable bar co-investors and later-stage funds can rely on. Our DNA report, tranche plan, and risk register double as the backbone of a structured, credible data room.

Early access, steady flow

We stay embedded in accelerators and incubators across the UK and Ireland, so we meet exceptional teams before rounds get crowded. Cohort cadence gives the fund predictable dealflow and founders honest, early dialogue—not a last-minute scramble.

Live portfolio rhythm

Near-real-time visibility across P&L, runway, pipeline, cost drift, and product velocity lets us course-correct weeks to months earlier than quarterly board cycles alone. Momentum gets amplified; issues get tackled before they compound—for operators and for LP reporting discipline.

Structured capital, less dilution

Milestone-based tranches are designed to unlock grants and accelerator capital alongside our commitment—putting more total capital to work in the company when the plan executes, without proportionally more dilution. LPs see efficiency; founders keep more ownership at the same risk stage.

Global operators & co-investors

Our network of operators, founders, funds, co-investors, advisors, and ecosystem partners opens design partners, pilots, talent, and follow-on conversations. That depth improves inbound quality across our programmes and strengthens every portfolio company’s path to the next round.
