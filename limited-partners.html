Our process

From conviction to capital deployment

A structured pathway that de-risks early-stage investment through measurable gates and operator-depth engagement at every stage.

Sourcing & screening

Deal origination through our accelerator network, founder pool, and ecosystem partnerships. Initial screening against our sector thesis and founder calibre bar.

DNA-level due diligence

Deep-tech, commercial, financial, and operational diligence that produces a structured DNA report — the backbone of a credible data room.

Investment Committee 4.0

Our enhanced IC process evaluates conviction through a Known + Unknown + Believe framework, producing clear investment KPIs tied to tranches.

Milestone-based deployment

Capital deployed in structured tranches designed to unlock grants and accelerator capital alongside our commitment—more total capital with less dilution.

Active portfolio management

Near-real-time monitoring, operator-depth support, and structured reporting cadence that course-corrects early and amplifies momentum.
